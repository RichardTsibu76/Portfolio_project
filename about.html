ABOUT US

Our church is dedicated to fostering a community of love, compassion, and faith. We base our beliefs and decisions on the word of God, which serves as our final authority in all matters. At our church, you'll find a welcoming congregation that seeks to follow the teachings of Jesus Christ. We offer various programs and ministries for people of all ages, including worship services, Bible studies, youth groups, and outreach initiatives. Join us as we journey together in faith, seeking to grow spiritually and make a positive impact in our world.

HISTORY

Pastor Richard is Young Ghanaian Pastor who operates under the Apostolic ministry , his ministry has touched many lives accross the globe. He is Man of prayer and intercession and has a great passion for the youth and the less privileged in the society. He teaches the word undilutedly with great passion and power. He is a single man who is very passionate about the things of God and has a great desire to see the church of God grow and become a force to reckon with in the society. He is also a Software Engineer by profession and has a great passion for technology and innovation. This area of his life has also enabled him to use technology together with the power of the Holyghost to reach out to many The ministry has many branches of about 400 all over the world and has a great vision to reach out to many more.
